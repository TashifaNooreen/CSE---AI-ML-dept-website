Department of CSE AI & ML

Events & Workshops

The Department of CSE AI & ML regularly organizes workshops, professional development programs, and technical events to enhance students' skills and knowledge. These events are often conducted in collaboration with professional societies like IEEE.

One Week Professional Development Program on Emerging Trends in Quantum Computing & Machine Learning

Conducted by: IEEE Professional Society

A week-long program designed to introduce participants to the latest advancements in quantum computing and its integration with machine learning. Attendees will explore quantum algorithms, quantum-inspired ML models, and their real-world applications.

Workshop on AI for Aerial Computer Vision in Drone Technology

Conducted by: IEEE Professional Society

A hands-on workshop focused on applying artificial intelligence to aerial computer vision systems used in drones. Participants will learn about object detection, navigation, and real-time data processing in drone technology.

One Week Professional Development Program on Recent Trends in Machine Learning and Data Analytics

Conducted by: IEEE Professional Society

A comprehensive program covering the latest trends in machine learning and data analytics. Topics include deep learning, big data tools, predictive modeling, and practical use cases in various industries.

One Day Project Expo - Parishodana 2.0

Conducted by: Department of CSE AI & ML

A one-day project expo showcasing innovative projects developed by students in the fields of AI and ML. Participants will present their work, receive feedback, and network with peers and faculty members.

5-day Professional Development Program on Emerging Trends in Machine Learning

Conducted by: IEEE Professional Society

A five-day intensive program exploring cutting-edge trends in machine learning. Topics include reinforcement learning, natural language processing, and generative AI, with hands-on sessions and case studies.

Two Days Hands-on Workshop on Understanding Routing and Switching Concepts in Data Communication and Networking using CICSO Packet Tracer

Conducted by: IEEE Professional Society

A two-day workshop focused on understanding routing and switching concepts in networking. Participants will use Cisco Packet Tracer to simulate and configure networks, gaining practical experience in data communication.

ATAL FDP on Internet of (IoD) Drones Applications and Futures

Conducted by: IEEE Professional Society

A faculty development program (FDP) exploring the Internet of Drones (IoD) and its applications. Topics include drone-based delivery systems, surveillance, and future advancements in drone technology.

Two-days Workshop on Building Scalable Network Application using NodeJS

Conducted by: IEEE Professional Society

A workshop focused on building scalable network applications using Node.js. Participants will learn about asynchronous programming, REST APIs, and deploying applications to cloud platforms.

Two-Day Hands-on Workshop on Ethical Hacking “Penetration Testing and Exploitation Tools”

Conducted by: IEEE Professional Society

A hands-on workshop teaching ethical hacking techniques, including penetration testing and exploitation tools. Participants will learn how to identify vulnerabilities and secure systems effectively.

CODIGO (Debug your C Knowledge) (Online Mode)

Conducted by: Department of CSE AI & ML

An online debugging competition designed to test and enhance participants' knowledge of the C programming language. Challenges include solving coding problems and debugging errors in real-time.

Two-Days Workshop on Project Development Methodologies

Conducted by: IEEE Professional Society

A workshop focused on methodologies for developing software projects. Topics include Agile, Scrum, Kanban, and best practices for managing project timelines and resources.

Emerging Technologies

Conducted by: Department of CSE AI & ML

A seminar or workshop introducing students to emerging technologies like blockchain, IoT, AR/VR, and their applications in various industries. Participants will gain insights into future career opportunities.

Life Cycle of Telecom

Conducted by: Department of CSE AI & ML

A session explaining the life cycle of telecommunications systems, from design and implementation to maintenance and upgrades. Participants will learn about key technologies like 5G and satellite communication.

Freshers' Event

The department hosts an annual Freshers' event to welcome new students and introduce them to the vibrant culture of the college. It includes fun activities, games, and performances.
